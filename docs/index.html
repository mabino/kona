Keep your Mac awake with Kona in your Dock.

Wake States

Create named profiles with custom settings to keep your system active exactly how you need it.

🖥️

Screen Control

Independently control screen dimming and system lock behavior for each profile.

📅

Scheduling

Set recurring schedules with specific days and time windows for automatic activation.

⏱️

Flexible Durations

Quickly choose from 15 minutes to 8 hours, or keep your Mac awake indefinitely.

☕

Menu Bar Access

One-click toggles directly from your menu bar. See status at a glance with the coffee cup icon.

🚀

Native Integration

Built with Swift and SwiftUI for macOS 14+. Supports open-at-login and auto-activation.
